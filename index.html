Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What types of projects do you work on?

We work on a wide range of projects including web applications, blockchain DApps, AI automation, infrastructure setup, and custom software solutions. Our expertise spans both traditional web2 and emerging web3 technologies.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex DApp or enterprise solution could take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! We offer various support packages including maintenance, updates, monitoring, and enhancement services. We're committed to the long-term success of your project.

What is your approach to security?

Security is paramount in everything we build. We follow industry best practices, conduct security audits, implement encryption, and use secure coding standards. For Web3 projects, we perform smart contract audits.

Can you help train our team on new technologies?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive training programs covering Web3, blockchain, AI/ML, DevOps, cybersecurity, and more. Training can be customized to your team's specific needs and skill levels.
